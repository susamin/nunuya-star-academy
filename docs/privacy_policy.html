🐱 隱私政策 / Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last Updated: 2026-06-08

1. 資料收集 / Data Collection

Nunuya Star Academy 不收集任何個人資料。
Nunuya Star Academy does not collect any personal data.

所有遊戲資料（等級、粉絲數、登入紀錄）僅儲存於您的裝置本地端，不會傳送至任何伺服器。
All game data (level, fans, login records) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

2. 第三方服務 / Third-Party Services

本應用程式目前不使用任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤服務。
This application currently does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

3. 兒童隱私 / Children's Privacy

本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童，且不收集任何兒童個人資料。
This application is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

4. 資料刪除 / Data Deletion

如需刪除遊戲資料，請至遊戲內設定頁選擇「重置資料」，或直接解除安裝本應用程式。
To delete your game data, use the "Reset Data" option in the in-game settings, or simply uninstall the application.

5. 政策變更 / Policy Changes

如有政策變更，將更新本頁面。
If this policy changes, we will update this page.
